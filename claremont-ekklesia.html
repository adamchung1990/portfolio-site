Claremont Ekklesia

The Claremont Ekklesia magazine is a student-run bi-annual publication that collects articles of Christian students at the Claremont Colleges, ranging from topics in the physical sciences to entertainment. As the lead designer and art director, I had to work within a very tight budget and timeline to create a look that could easily repeated with future student-led teams and could be made cheaply, while looking anything but. I coordinated a team of photographers to take photos of the surrounding area to give our magazine a distinctively local feel. Publications are still being made every semester.
